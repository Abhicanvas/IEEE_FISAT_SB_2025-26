About IEEE Robotics and Automation Society Student Branch Chapter FISAT.

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) functions as a professional society within the IEEE community. RAS is committed to enhancing both the theoretical and practical aspects of robotics and automation engineering. As one of the IEEE's largest and most vibrant societies, RAS is critical to facilitating knowledge exchange and collaboration among academics, engineers, and professionals in this field. Its purpose is to encourage technological developments, support research dissemination, and push innovation in robotics and automation.
